Use Case:

Title: Finding a new trail for a workout.

Description: Kim searches the app for a new trail for her daily run.

User Name & Role: Kim Gallegos; avid runner who enjoys running on new trails

Usage Preconditions: Smartphone, cell service, must be logged in to her Trails account

Usage Postconditions: Runs on the trail and leaves a rating

Interaction Flow:

  • Kim signs into ABQTrails.com
  • Server returns the site home page - a list of popular trails, a map below that, and a search bar
  • Kim clicks on the map to see trails near hear
  • Server returns the map page, which zooms in on her location, and show her pins of trails near her
  • Kim selects a pin near her
  • Server returns information about that trail - photo, rating, difficulty, etc
  • Kim decides to run on that trail
  • After she finishes her run, she rates the trail, and shares her experience on social media
  • Server store the rating to her profile, and shares post with social media app

    • Frequency of Use: Multiple times per week
