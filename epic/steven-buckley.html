User Story

As a user, Steven would like to have some entertaining background noise.

Use Case

Title: Acquiring a non-distracting form of entertainment.

Description: Michael wants to listen/watch a form of entertainment that he is interested in.

Name of user & their role: Michael; a developer who would prefer some non-intrusive entertainment to listen/watch while he preforms menial tasks.

Usage Preconditions: Must have had some prior knowledge of Twitch.tv and an idea of something that he might be interested in listening to or watching

Usage post conditions: Michael is being entertained and has the option to follow or subscribe to the streamer that he is currently streaming.

Interaction Flow:

  • Micheal enter twitch.tv into his web browser.
  • The server returns Twitch's home page.
  • While on the home page, the home page will automatically begin streaming a featured stream as well as listing other featured streams.
  • Michael is not satisfied with the featured streams, he then scrolls down to view streams that are currently popular by viewer count and also separated by console.
  • Micheal is still not satisfied with his current selection, he then scrolls to the top of the page and clicks the text box with the icon of a magnifying glass.
  • Upon doing so Micheal is able to type in the text either the name of a streamer that is currently online or the name of a game that he would be interested in watch.
  • Michael has chosen to type in the name of a game that he is interested in watching and hit search.
  • Twitch will change the page and begin listing all the streamers that are currently streaming that particular game.
  • Michael passively watches and listens to the stream while doing other task
  • Micheal exits out satisfied

Frequency of Use: 3 to 4 times per week.
