User Persona:

  • Name: Steven Buckley
  • Age: 27
  • Occupation: Waiter
  • Hobbies: Playing video games, going on walks every couple of weeks, and reading
  • Technology: Phone: Samsung Galaxy S8. Laptop: HP Pavilion 15t. Console: Nintendo Switch and Playstation 4
  • Attitude: Easy-going, Casual, and laid-back
  • Needs/Goals/Features:
    • A way to quickly to look up information necessary to on a walk
    • Perhaps a way to rate the trail he walked on to allow others to know how good or bad it was
  • Frustrations:
    • Is tired of sites that give too much information in a a large amount of words
    • Wants something fast and easy to use while on the go

User Story:

As a user, Steven would like to quickly look up some information regarding trails in a easy to understand method.

Use Case:

Title: Going on an enjoyable walk.

Description: Looking up some information to go on a walk that would be appropriate for a walker on of his level.

Name of user & their role: Steven; a waiter who would like to go on a nice walk.

Usage Preconditions: Must have some prior knowledge of the application, a device to access the application and an internet connection

Usage post conditions: Steven has gone on a nice trail and has left his review.

Interaction Flow:

  • Steven boots up the site on his phone.
  • The server returns the site's home page.
  • While on the home page, the home page will automatically ask Steven to log in.
  • Steven enters his log in information.
  • Steven begins to search for trails by location and difficulty.
  • The server returns a list of choices and Steven chooses one from the list .
  • Server display more information about the trail in an easy to understand manner.
  • Steven acquires the location and heads to it.
  • Steven goes on a walk
  • After Steven has finished his walk he chooses to leave a review
  • The server receives his review and adds it to the database
  • Steve closes the site.

Frequency of Use: Once every 2-3 weeks.
