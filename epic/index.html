Purpose

To give users an interactive, fun way to find trails that they can use for a variety of recreational activities, with the goal of encouraging more people to enjoy outdoor activity.

Audience

The audience will be very general and include persons from all over the Albuquerque area who enjoy or want to enjoy the generous outdoor facilities, parks, and trails that this area offers.

Goal

We want people to enjoy hiking, biking, fishing, camping, and any other type of outdoor activity, and provide a way that local residents can easily find a trail or site that accommodates said activity.
